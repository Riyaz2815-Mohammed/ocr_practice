→ Limited information shared through friends or social media
→ Students ensure about how to access facilities.
→ Occasional announcements without follow-up guidance.
→ Students focus only on exams due to uncertainty.

## Observations:

→ students hesitate to explore available resources
→ Facilities exist but are not actively used.
→ No single platform for information.

## Emotions:

→ Frustrated
→ Unmotivated

→ Clear communication about facilities & benefits
→ Simple registration & access process.
→ Faculty guidance & student mentors
→ Regular awareness programs & demonstrations

## Grains:

→ Increased usage of libraries, labs & training programs.
→ Improved confidence & motivation among students.
